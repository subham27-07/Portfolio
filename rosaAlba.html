Rosa Alba Resort, Vietnam

The Resort concept of Rosa Alba, Tuy Hoa is providing physical and mental treatment for the long stay guests by the nature friendly environment and healthy activities such as spa, yoga, beach sports and so on. To achieve a perfect atmosphere that enables the guests a maximum body and mind purification and relaxation, the resort is designed by harmonious mix of greenery, natural stone and bamboo. The design mission is to harmonize a hotel, 40 Shophouse, 14 VIP villas and other 65 villas. The roads start from the entrance to the seaside thoroughly connecting every service areas and bringing fresh and gracious breath of sea breeze to the internal space.

To enrich the experience, all the residential plots/Villas are placed around a water body . The river at the rear end of the site and surrounding trees will create cool atmosphere by wind visualization, vapor effect and transpiration with providing shade under trees.
