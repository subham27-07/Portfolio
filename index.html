Hi, I am Subham

Hi, I'm Subham! I am a Computational Designer with a background in architecture. I love building products that engage and educate people. I am studying Design Computation at the University of North Carolina, Charlotte, where I am working on interdisciplinary projects like a Social Media Data Exploration app that helps Data Analysts and Social Scientists to understand social media data. I have also designed and developed a cellular IoT Platform for Cyber-Physical Interaction between humans. Worked on analyzing the relationship between the Cost and Demand of shared Mobility Systems (On-Demand mobility systems).

My work style makes me oscillate between two selves - on one end, I think deeply about humans, their emotions, their desires - akin more to a poet and a philosopher. On another end, I think like a mathematician, thinking about the right tech framework, software architecture, and user interface. My end goal is always to build products that have empathy engineered into them. They are able to translate a fundamental insight about human beings into a solid tech product people love to use.
