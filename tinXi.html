Tin-Xi Bamboo Restaurant China

Ting-Xi bamboo restaurant is situated in a suburban area surrounded by lush forestry near the center of xiamen, an exciting coastal city in china. the functional requirements primarily consists of a restaurant and a café lounge which seats 200 guests. Additionally, the internal area is designed to be a flexible space that can host many different types of events.

I was involed in detail design and resolving the construction details for the project.
