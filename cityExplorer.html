Urban City Explorer-URBAN Trails

No source of information can be as effective as expericence. Apart from one’s personal experience gained by other people can also play a major role in collecting data. Urbam trails uses this principle to give great user experience interms of experience. The app follows four basic principles:
1. Exploration of various travel experiences.
2. Information collection and assimilation from other peoples experiences.
3. Sharing personal experiences.
4. Connecting peoples.
The app will prove to be a great platform for architects, urban designers, artists, jornalists and photographers to increase their feild of vision.
